CALISTHENICS: 1-YEAR ZERO → PRO PLAN (Tailored)
Age: 32
Current level:
- Pull-ups: 2
- Push-ups: 10
- Dips: 4
Equipment: Pull-up bar
Goal priority: ALL (strength + skills)

============================================================
END-OF-YEAR TARGETS (realistic if consistent)
- Pull-ups: 10–15 strict
- Dips: 15–25 strict
- Push-ups: 40–60 strict
- Handstand: 30–60s wall + 5–20s freestanding
- Muscle-up: strong prep → likely first clean rep
- Front lever: solid advanced tuck holds/negatives
============================================================

WEEKLY STRUCTURE (Year-round)
Best option: 5 days/week
Day 1: Push + Handstand
Day 2: Pull + Core (Lever)
Day 3: Legs + Conditioning + Mobility
Day 4: Push/Pull Mix + Skills
Day 5: Pull Power + Core (Muscle-up pathway later)
Days 6–7: Rest / light walk + stretching

If you can only do 4 days/week: remove Day 4.

SESSION RULES
- Most sets: stop with 1–2 reps in reserve (no grinding).
- Skills are practice, not max effort.
- Every 4th week: deload to ~70% of your normal sets.

------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 1 (Weeks 1–8): Build volume + perfect form
Goal: fast improvement in basics + joint resilience.

DAY 1 — PUSH + HANDSTAND (45–60 min)
1) Dips: 5×3 (clean depth)
2) Push-ups: 4×6–8
3) Pike push-ups: 4×5–8
4) Wall handstand hold: 8 min total (20–40s sets)
5) Scapular push-ups: 2×12–15

DAY 2 — PULL + CORE (LEVER)
1) Pull-ups: 6×1–2 (perfect reps only)
2) Negative pull-ups (5 sec down): 4×2
3) Dead hang: 3×30–45s
4) Hollow hold: 4×20–30s
5) Tuck front lever hold (even tiny): 6×8–12s
   (Alternative: tuck rows if holds are too hard)

DAY 3 — LEGS + CONDITIONING
- Split squats: 4×8–12/leg
- Squats: 3×15–25
- Calf raises: 4×15–25
- Conditioning: 15–25 min brisk walk/jog/cycle
- Mobility: 10 min (hips/ankles/hamstrings)

DAY 4 — MIX + SKILL
- Push-ups: 3×AMRAP (leave 2 reps)
- Pull-ups: 5×1–2
- Hanging knee raises: 4×8–12
- Handstand practice: 10–15 min
- Shoulder band work (if available): 2×20

DAY 5 — PULL POWER + CORE
1) Chin-ups: 5×2–4 (use small jump if needed; control down)
2) Explosive pull-up intent: 6×1 (pull fast, higher each week)
3) Scapular pulls: 3×10
4) Hanging knee → leg raise progression: 4×6–10
5) Side plank: 3×30–45s/side

WEEK 4 DELOAD
- Reduce volume to ~70% of sets.

PHASE 1 MILESTONES (by Week 8)
- Pull-ups: 4–6
- Dips: 8–10
- Push-ups: 20–25
- Better handstand wall control

------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 2 (Weeks 9–20): Strength + real skill progress
Key upgrade: add a backpack for weighted work once:
- Pull-ups reach 6+ strict AND
- Dips reach 10+ strict (clean)

DAY 1 — PUSH + HANDSTAND
- Dips: 6×4–6
- Push-ups (harder variation): 4×8–12
- Pike push-ups: 5×6–10
- Wall handstand: 10–12 min total
- Handstand wall shoulder taps: 4×10–20 taps

DAY 2 — PULL + CORE (LEVER)
- Pull-ups: 6×3–5
- Top holds (chin above bar): 4×10–20s
- Slow negatives: 3×3
- Tuck front lever: 8–12 min total
- Hanging leg raises: 4×6–10

DAY 5 — PULL POWER
- High pull-up attempts (chest-to-bar intent): 6×2–3
- Pull-up volume: 4×AMRAP (leave 2 reps)
- Core (leg raises / hollow)

PHASE 2 MILESTONES (by Week 20)
- Pull-ups: 8–10
- Dips: 12–15
- Push-ups: 30–40
- Stronger wall handstand + better alignment

------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 3 (Weeks 21–36): Muscle-up pathway + freestanding handstand
Muscle-up checklist:
- Pull-ups: 10+ strict
- Chest-to-bar attempts: 8+ (or close)
- Dips: 15+
- Strong core + transition drills

DAY 5 becomes MU PREP DAY
1) Explosive pull-ups: 8×2
2) Straight bar dip (if possible) OR regular dips: 6×4–8
3) Transition drill (band-assisted MU or jumping MU): 10 min
4) Negative muscle-up (very slow): 5×1
5) Hanging leg raises / toes-to-bar progression: 4×6–10

HANDSTAND PROGRESSION (2×/week, on Day 1 and Day 4)
- Wall holds → wall shoulder taps → nose-to-wall alignment
- Then: freestanding kick-ups + balance drills
- Total: 15–25 minutes per skill session

PHASE 3 MILESTONES (by Week 36)
- Strong MU attempts (first rep possible for many)
- Freestanding handstand “moments” begin

------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 4 (Weeks 37–52): Pro polish + PRs
Structure:
- 2 strength days (push/pull)
- 2 skill days (handstand + MU/lever)
- 1 legs + conditioning day

STRENGTH PUSH (1×/week)
- Weighted dips: 6×3–5
- Handstand push-up progression: 5×3–6
- Planche lean: 6×15–25s

STRENGTH PULL (1×/week)
- Weighted pull-ups: 6×3–5
- High pulls: 6×2–4
- Front lever work: 10–15 min total

------------------------------------------------------------
DAILY 8-MIN JOINT ARMOR (before training)
- Wrist circles + palm pulses: 1 min
- Scapular push-ups: 2×10
- Dead hang: 1×30–45s
- Shoulder external rotation (band if available): 2×15
- Hip opener: 1 min

------------------------------------------------------------
PROGRESSION RULES (simple)
- Pull-ups: when you can do 6×2 clean, move to 5×3 next.
- Dips: when you can do 5×5, move to 6×5; then add backpack weight.
- Push-ups: build to 4×12; then upgrade variation (decline/diamond/archer).

RECOVERY (critical for “all goals”)
- Sleep: 7–9 hours
- Protein: ~1.6–2.2 g per kg bodyweight/day
- Steps: 7–10k/day
- Every 4th week: deload
